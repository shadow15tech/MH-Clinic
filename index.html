FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Answers To Your Spine Care Questions

We understand that spine problems can be overwhelming. Here are answers to some of the most common questions patients ask before visiting Dr. Hitesh N. Modi.

Do all spine problems require surgery?

+

No. Many spine conditions can be managed with non-surgical treatments. Surgery is recommended only when it offers the safest and most effective solution for your condition.

When should I consult a spine specialist?

+

If your neck pain, back pain, numbness, weakness, or sciatica persists for several weeks or affects your daily activities, it's advisable to consult a spine specialist for a detailed evaluation.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

+

Please bring your previous medical records, MRI or CT scan films and reports, X-rays, prescriptions, and any recent blood test reports related to your condition.

How long is the recovery after spine surgery?

+

Recovery varies depending on the type of surgery and your overall health. Many patients begin walking within a short time after surgery, while complete recovery may take several weeks or months with proper rehabilitation.

Do you perform minimally invasive spine surgery?

+

Yes. Dr. Hitesh N. Modi specializes in minimally invasive and endoscopic spine surgery whenever it is clinically appropriate, helping eligible patients benefit from smaller incisions and faster recovery.

Need Personal Guidance?

Our team is here to answer your questions, review your reports, and help you understand the most appropriate treatment options for your spine condition.
